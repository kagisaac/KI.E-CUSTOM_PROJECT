Export Filing Platform: Export declarations in the fast lane


You can’t export anything without filing an electronic customs declaration.
But that’s no reason to keep your customers waiting for their goods.
Export Filing from AEB automates and accelerates your export processes
a scalable solution that helps new exporters and seasoned professionals alike.
For just one country – or more. In one platform.

Import Filing Platform: Control and speed for import declarations


Either you hire a service provider to clear your imports through customs
or you keep your money and insource the expertise with Import Filing
from AEB. The software offers intuitive, step-by-step guidance through
the entire process of declaring your imports. The processing speed and security
of your import management will be hard to beat. For just one country – or more. In one platform.

EMCS Filing: Fast and reliable EMCS processes


Excise duties are not levied until goods have reached the place where they will be consumed.
Anyone who transports excise goods under suspension of excise duty
is required to participate in the electronic EMCS procedure.
EMCS Filing turns a chore into a pleasure by automating data entry
and providing smart assistance and meticulous documentation.

Origin & Preferences: Seize the full potential of free trade

Origin & Preferences from KI.E-Custom lets you take advantage of the benefits and
opportunities EU, UK and Swiss free trade agreements. Save money on customs duties
and help your customers do the same making your products more competitive in the process.
The Origin & Preferences software from KI.E-Custom ensures you have the necessary proofs
and helps you reliably assess the preferential status of your own products and those you purchase
in different locations.
